Beginner Tutorial

1
Buy a seed

Open Seed Shop and buy Carrot seeds first. The button selects the seed after buying it.

2
Plant it

Click an empty soil plot. The selected seed appears above the garden so you know what will be planted.

3
Wait for ready

Watch the progress bar. A glowing plot is ready to harvest.

4
Harvest and sell

Click the ready plot, then use Inventory to sell crops or Sell All for quick coins.

5
Grow faster

Use coins on better seeds, plot upgrades, gear, pets, and daily quests. Events and rebirths are late-game goals.
